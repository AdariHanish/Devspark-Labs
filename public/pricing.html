Frequently Asked Questions

Why is advance payment required?

The ‚Çπ500 advance payment confirms your booking and allows us to allocate resources for your project. It's deducted from your final amount, so you only pay the remaining balance on delivery.

Do you provide code explanation for viva?

Yes! This is our specialty. We provide detailed code explanation sessions via call/screen share to help you understand your project completely and confidently answer viva questions.

We offer revisions based on your plan. Minor changes are usually free, and we support you until your final submission. Major scope changes may have additional charges.

How do I make payment?

We accept UPI payments. You can pay via our QR code on the payment page and upload the screenshot for confirmation. We'll verify and confirm your booking within 1 hour.

Mini projects: 5-7 days | Major projects: 1-2 weeks | Custom projects: 2-4 weeks. Urgent delivery is available at 20% extra cost.
